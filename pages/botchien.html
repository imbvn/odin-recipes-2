Bot Chien

Description

Saigon's favorite streetside snack, bot chien, is popular with both the afterschool and the after-midnight crowd. Chunk of rice flour dough are fried in a large wok until crispy and then an egg is broken into the mix.

Ingredients

  • 1 bag of rice flour
  • 3 eggs beaten
  • 3 scallions sliced
  • Some papaya sliced
  • 3 tbsp cornstarch
  • Salt, sugar
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 7 cups of water
  • 2 tbsp vinegar
  • Soy sauce or fish sauce

Instructions

  1. Heat a pot of water to steam the cake. Once hot water, pour mixture in a pan and steam for 30-40 minutes until done.
  2. When insert a chopstick in and it comes out clean then it is done.
  3. Cut into 1-inch by 2-inch rectangles rice flour.
  4. Use a bit of oil to fry the rectangles on both sides until golden brown and crispy.
  5. Add 3 sliced scallions. After that, add 3 beaten eggs. Break up the egg a bit and stir-fry so the rice flour absorbs the sauce.
  6. Mix everything together and stir. Continue frying both sides
