Instant Pot Pho Recipe

Description

This Vietnamese staple consisting of a salty broth, fresh rice noodles, a sprinkling of herbs and chicken or beef, features predominately in the local diet - and understandably so. It's cheap, tasty, and widely available at all hours. In this article, we will be covering a simpler version using an Instant Pot!

  • 3.2 lbs bone marrow
  • 2 onions
  • 1 ginger
  • Pho spices package (Old Man Que Huong brand)
  • Seasonings:
    • Salt
    • Black Peppercorn
    • Sugar
    • Fish Sauce
    • Chicken Bouillon Powder
  • Pho Toppings:
    • Beef Brisket
    • Beef Tendon
    • Beef Raw Slices
    • Beef Balls
